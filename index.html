    FAQ

    What is the service that you provide?

    Answer: We connect property managers, real estate developers, facilities managers, and other professionals with a network of personally vetted construction companies for their construction needs

    Who are the construction companies in your network?

    Answer: Our network consists of a variety of construction companies that have been personally vetted for their quality of work, reliability, and professionalism. They specialize in a range of construction services, from residential to commercial and industrial projects.

    How do you vet the construction companies in your network?

    Answer: We have a rigorous vetting process that includes checking references, reviewing past projects, and conducting interviews. We also continuously monitor the performance of the companies in our network to ensure they maintain our high standards.

    How can a construction company join your network?

    Answer: Construction companies interested in joining our network can apply through our website. We have a rigorous vetting process to ensure that only the most reliable and high-quality companies join our network. After successful vetting, you'll be part of our network and we'll connect you with clients that match your expertise and service offerings.

    How much does your service cost?

    Answer: The cost of our service depends on the specifics of your construction needs. Please contact us for a personalized quote.

    What if I'm not satisfied with the construction company you connected me with?

    Answer: We strive to ensure the satisfaction of our clients. If you're not satisfied with a construction company, please let us know and we'll work to resolve the issue, which may include connecting you with a different company.
